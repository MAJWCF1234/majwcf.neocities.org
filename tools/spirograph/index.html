About This Generator

Welcome to the Advanced Retro Spirograph Generator! This tool allows you to create mesmerizing spirograph patterns with a retro aesthetic. Customize various parameters, choose from multiple modes, and export your creations in different formats.

Features:

  • Multiple spirograph modes including Hypotrochoid, Epitrochoid, Rose, and Lissajous Curves.
  • Real-time parameter adjustments with live previews.
  • Layer management for complex designs.
  • Save and manage custom presets.
  • Export drawings as PNG, SVG, and PDF.
  • Responsive design for use on any device.
